David Goggins.

What makes a hero? A hero to me is someone who inspires others around them to do better.

To me, the person who inspires me to push past my limits and the expectations of others is David Goggins, a former Navy Seal who embodies the fact of never giving up. His motivational talks drive something in the people listening to him, he's not for everyone but he will tell you cold cut what you need to do to succeed. He ran a marathon nonstop, broke both of his shins and kept going for so many miles and never quit, he embodies the thinking mind over matter and that in itself is inspiring.
